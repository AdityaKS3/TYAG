Our Organisation TYAG is a non- profit registered Cultural Organisation established as on 01-September-2003 as a registered societies (Non-Government) in Bihar. The main objective of the Organisation is to develop the folk culture and research as well as to preserve and protect Indian culture in the rural area by organize Drama (Natak), workshop, Seminar, Symposia and rallies etc. and also organise the folk Indian culture like dance, drama and music etc.

THE MOTIVE OF TYAG IS Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries, Aged/Elderly, Agriculture, Art & Culture, Children, Civic Issues, Differently Abled, Disaster Management, Dalit Upliftment, Drinking Water, Education & Literacy, Environment & Forests, Food Processing, Health & Family Welfare, HIV/AIDS, Legal Awareness & Aid, Labour & Employment, Micro Finance (SHGs), Minority Issues, Micro Small & Medium Enterprises, Nutrition, Panchayati Raj, Right to Information & Advocacy, Rural Development & Poverty Alleviation, Sports IN BIHAR->Patna, Saran, Vaishali

  • TYAG is a registered non-profit cultural organisation founded by the educationalist, cultural devotees and reputed person of the society.
  • The organisation is trying to create awareness among the rural people through cultural activities. The organisation is trying to bring these people to main stream of the society by directly engaging them as performing activities in the programs. The main aim of the organisation is the overall cultural development in the state.
  • Hence to maintain our performing activities very impressive to the people, the organisation needs the financial assistance from the government for the overall development of the artists.
