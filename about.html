Objective

 The main aims and objective of the TYAG is Social Justice and empowerment, formulation and participation in project which are educative in nature and works of social transformation through justice, love and human dignity, co-operation and peace, innovative research collaboration and to initiate and strengthen institutional links to arrange and organize antique show, art show, audio and video show, book writing publication, car rally, charity premier show, newspaper, brochure, matches, dance event, fancy dress competition, fashion show, fete, film shows, foundation or endowment award and prizes, greetings card exhibition, magic and civics show, musical concerts, placement assistance, printing calendar, scholarships, promote small saving, Thematic puppet shows, tour and picnics, Toy libraries, yoga classes, judo classes, pollution and waste management including legal assistance, initiation against pollution, environment research and action, urban planning public services and civics amenities.

Past Experience in cultural Activities
